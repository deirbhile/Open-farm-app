About Us

Annacarty Open Farm is a new Petting Farm on the Tuohy farm.

This farm has been in the family for over three generations!

We are a dairy farm that mainly consists of Holstein Fresians.

Our farm is located 13.5km from Tipperary, and 40km from Limerick.

Annacarty RIC Barracks is located on the farm!

Our farm has seating areas, a playground, and much more!
